Attack On Titan

Attack on Titan follows Eren Yeager, a young boy who vows to eliminate the Titans after they destroy his hometown and kill his mother. Joining the Scout Regiment alongside his friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert, Eren discovers shocking truths about the Titans, the history of humanity, and the world beyond the walls. As alliances shift and battles grow more brutal, Eren's quest for freedom leads him down a dark and devastating path that will determine the fate of humanity.
