AVENGERS

Discover the legacy of the big 3.

Steve Rogers | Captain America

A symbol of courage and conviction, Steve Rogers rose from frailty to become the living embodiment of freedom. With his unbreakable shield in hand, Captain America led the charge against tyranny, never wavering in his pursuit of justice. His legacy? A hero who always stands for what's right, even when it means standing alone. The heart and soul of the Avengers, Cap united heroes under a single cause, championing freedom and honor no matter the cost.

Anthony Edward Stark | Iron Man

Tony Stark, the genius who built an empire, transformed himself into Iron Man—a self-made hero. His legacy isn’t just his armor but the man inside: a visionary who sacrificed everything to protect the world. His brilliance saved the universe, but his heart saved humanity. His final act, a single snap, rewrote destiny—proving that his greatest creation was the sacrifice of his own life.

Thor Odinson | Thor God of Thunder

From the heart of Asgard to the ends of the cosmos, Thor, the God of Thunder, wielded Mjolnir with divine fury. His legacy is that of a warrior-king, battling gods and monsters alike, but always striving to be worthy. Thor’s strength lies not just in his might but in his heart, leading him to protect both gods and mortals. In his darkest moments, Thor rose again, proving that true strength comes not from his hammer, but from the heart of a king willing to fight for all.
